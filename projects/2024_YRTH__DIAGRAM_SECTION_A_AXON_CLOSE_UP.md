## Diagram role
Sectional representation of a multi-level spatial arrangement

## Spatial focus
- Vertical circulation
- Floor-to-ceiling enclosure
- Ground-level platform

## Diagrammatic ideas
- Layered plane articulation
- Structural framing as spatial divider
- Depth through linear perspective

## Reading
The diagram establishes a clear hierarchy of spatial planes through the contrast of thick, continuous black lines against finer grid lines. The vertical circulation element acts as both a structural and spatial anchor, defining transitions between levels while maintaining visual continuity. Enclosure is rendered through a rhythmic pattern of vertical elements that modulate the sense of permeability. The ground-level platform is presented as a distinct, supported plane, establishing a clear separation from the upper volumes. The overall composition emphasizes the interplay between solid and void, using line weight to direct attention to key spatial thresholds and structural relationships.
