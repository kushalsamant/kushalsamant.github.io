## Diagram role
Axonometric representation of structural framing

## Spatial focus
- Ground plane and supporting structure
- Vertical enclosure system
- Internal spatial division

## Diagrammatic ideas
- Emphasis on structural hierarchy
- Grid-based organization of elements
- Layering of enclosure and support

## Reading
The diagram establishes a clear vertical articulation through the differentiation of load-bearing elements and enclosure. The grid on the floor plane anchors the spatial organization, while the vertical fins create a rhythmic pattern that defines the building’s envelope. The support structure is rendered as a distinct, articulated system that elevates the main volume, creating a visual separation between the building and its ground plane. The internal voids suggest spatial modulation without detailing interior functions. The overall composition prioritizes structural logic over surface treatment, using line density to imply materiality and assembly.
