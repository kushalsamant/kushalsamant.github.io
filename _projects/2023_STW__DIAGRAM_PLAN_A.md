## Spatial focus

- Service core placement

## Diagrammatic ideas
- Layered spatial hierarchy

## Reading
The diagram establishes a clear vertical axis through the central core, which anchors circulation and service functions. Spatial zones are defined through varying densities of filled and outlined forms, suggesting differentiated programmatic priorities. The grid structure underpins the layout, providing a consistent spatial reference while allowing for irregular insertions that interrupt the uniformity. Thresholds are marked by abrupt shifts in line weight and form, indicating transitions between public and private realms. The composition balances enclosed volumes with open circulation, creating a rhythm that guides movement through the space.
